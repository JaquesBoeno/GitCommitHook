- Text Colors:

  - \033[30m: Black
  - \033[31m: Red
  - \033[32m: Green
  - \033[33m: Yellow
  - \033[34m: Blue
  - \033[35m: Magenta
  - \033[36m: Cyan
  - \033[37m: White

- Text Styles:
  -\033[1m: Bold
  -\033[2m: Faint or dim
  -\033[3m: Italic
  -\033[4m: Underline
  -\033[5m: Blinking
  -\033[7m: Inverted colors (swap foreground and background)
  -\033[8m: Hidden

- Reset Formatting:
  - \033[0m: Reset all styles and colors
